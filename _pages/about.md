# Biography

I am a <strong style="color: #0070C0;">Senior Engineer</strong> at the School of Physics, Northeast Normal University (NENU). I specialize in (<strong style="color: #0070C0;">infrared and visible light image fusion technology</strong>), (<strong style="color: #0070C0;">image processing</strong>), and (<strong style="color: #0070C0;">physiological signal pattern recognition</strong>). 

In addition to my research activities, I am also an educator passionate about technical practice. I have participated in multiple interdisciplinary projects, combining physical experimental techniques with modern information technology to enhance the effectiveness of experimental teaching and students’ research capabilities.
